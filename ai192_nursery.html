Nursery Rhymes

This is my Nursery Rhyme project of the Humpty Dumpty rhyme. We were supposed to design some scenes of the Humpty Dumpty nursery rhyme that were created unusually.

I've worked on my project with my sister Becky. Becky and her boyfriend (later husband) Aaron made a design of six scenes. We've taken some pictures of our Lego pieces and board and we've traced the photos of them.

To view the scenes, click on the number buttons.
