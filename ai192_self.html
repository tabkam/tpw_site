Self Portrait

I used a bright light to take a picture of myself. On Photoshop, I posterized the picture of myself, so I could draw lines where the colros changed. I opened my self portrait pictures on Illustrator, and I drew a line where the colors changed. I used an Eyedropper Tool and I selected the colro of posterized colors, and I filled in the areas between the lines. I created a background by using a light to dark radial gradient on it.
