Shopper DigiPainting

This is my project of my eldest sister Becky going shopping in th Albertsons grocery store. I used the Marquee Tool to draw an oval marquee, I inverse selected it, and deleted it. I created the layers for each color, I used the Eyedropper Tool to pick a color, and I painted the same color to refill it on each layer. My Dad help me take a pictre of a fast food stuff. I deleted an oer of the fast food stuff. I used the opacity and the Eraser Tool to erase the corner of the oval.

To see my final project that I did, move the mouse on the photo of my sister Becky.
