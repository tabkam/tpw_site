Dynamic Text

This animated project shows Billy Patrick Muddington sitting on a chair watching the airplane with a banner fly by through the window. I drew it in Illustrator, and I imported them into Animate. Then I animated the airplane with a motion tween. I've added the dynamic text onto the banner of the airplane. I've movie clip buttons to change the language into English, Spanish and French, and to restart the animation.
