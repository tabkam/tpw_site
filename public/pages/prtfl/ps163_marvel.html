Marvel Saga Comic Book Pages

These are the Marvel Saga comic book pages that I've colorized. First I scanned in the pages, and removed all its original colors. I used the Marquee Tool, and the Lasso Tool to select the color and deleted them. Some of the areas were not enclosed, because lines were separated apart. So I redrew them with using the Line Tool. Using separated laters to separated the colors, I selected the color swatches from the swatches palette.
