Alliterative Alphabetical Postcard

This is my Alliterative Alphabetical Postcard project, which starts with the letter "D". My Professor Chris Nielsen gave everyone a different letter, and asked them to create an alliterative alphabet postcard and illustrate it. I chose "Don't Destroy Ducky" because I like rubber ducks. I downloaded a picture of a rubber duck from the Internate, and I traced it. I created the red letter "D" as a "no" symbol by creating outlines on the typem created a diagonal rectangle and click on the "Add" pathfinder. I used 3D Extrude and Bevel on all the text. I creatd the waves by creating many smooth-point zigzags with different numbers of ridges. I created a background by creating a rectangle with a gradient to look like a horizon.
