X-Men Comic Book Pages

This is my project of the Uncanny X-Men comic book cover issue #136 with pages 1 and 15. I created the layers for each color. I used the Magic Wand and Lasso Tools and the Quick Mask to fill the colors into each white spot. I used the Gradient Tool to fill the background layer of the cover page into the pink and white gradient. On pages 1 and 15, I downloaded a picture of flame from the Internet, and I used it as a background for them.

To see my final project that I did, move the mouse on the black and white pages.
