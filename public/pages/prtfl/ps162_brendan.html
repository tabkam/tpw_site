Brendan Collage

This is my project of my cousin Brendan Hoolihan drinking with his straw. I tore a paper into pieces, and I scanned it in. I used the "Posterize" to sketch the lines on the image following the colors. I numbered the colors. I used the Magic Wand Tool on the piece of paper and I cut and paste it in different piece to fill in the colors.

To see my final project that I did, move the mouse on the photo of my cousin Brendan.
