Jackson Taylor Animations

This is my animated project in which I selected one of the three buttons, that lets my preteen human boy character Jackson Taylor catch the baseball including low, middle, high. I have used the bone tool to create Jackson's bone animation that lets him catch the ball lowly, in the middle and highly. I have also the animated background in which the clouds floats through the stage of this project, during Jackson's baseball catch, I've animated the birds flying through this stage, and I have used JavaScript to program buttons that executes Jackson's ball catches.
