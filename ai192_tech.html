Technical Drawing

This is my Technical Drawing project of the Panasonic Satellite Radio/CD Player with JRC GPS Receiver. The reason why I chose this is so people can listen to the radio on their CD players with better reception.

I took our CD player apart so I could take pictures of the inside and outside of it I drew a top view and a cutout view, and the bottom view. I added a JRC GPS receiver to the CD player. I added a description and technical details of the JRC GPS receiver.
