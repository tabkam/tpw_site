Realistic Turtle Ad

This is my project of my anthtopomorphized tortoise washing his tortoise shell. My Dad helped me take photos of my brother-in-law Aaron's tortoise, and he took photos ofme washing his car. I removed my head, legs and arms on my body. I cut the legs and head of the tortoise and pasted them on my body. I added them to my picture of the driveway. I put a tortoise shell on the driveway and I put some shadows on the tortoise shell and tortoise body.

To see my final project that I did, move the mouse on the photo of me washing the car.
